#+title: Baked Salmon

* With cream, dill and mustard

** Ingridients
- Salmon fillet with the skin (600-800 g for 3-4 ppl)
- Heavy cream 30% (400ml)
- Unsalted butter
- Shallot, minced
- Lemon (zest and juice)
- Fresh dill
- Dijon mustard (1.5-2 tbsp)
- Sea salt
- Pepper
- Whole grain mustard (optional)

** Instructions
- Prepare the oven -- 180 deg with fan or 190 static
- Put salmon in a baking tray, foil wrap is good. Season with salt and pepper
In a pan:
- Sweat shallot with butter
- Add cream and wait 3-4 min until thick
- Mix with mustard and zest of 1 lemon
- Adjust saltiness

Take off the heat, add fresh dill.

Pour sauce over salmon and bake 15+ minutes until done, depending on the thickness.

Serve with lemon juice and more fresh dill
